Job Recommender with Skills and Syllabus Matching

JR is a web application developed to help graduates from university to find a suitable job for them. Using syllabus learned in university, JR match graduates with job options available online. JR will rank the result accordingly for you to choose the most perfect job that require your skills. 

By using JR, user can also see  jobs that are not suitable for them (not matched with job requirements). Users can determine the lack of skills that they are having and learn more skills to improve theirself to be more competitive.
